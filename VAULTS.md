# The Genesis Vaults

> *Eight Vaults built PROJECT FORGE. All are dormant or partially active. All are still running.*

---

## PROJECT FORGE

Commissioned when it became clear that industrial pollution had passed the point of human-manageable cleanup. The goal: engineer biomechanical organisms capable of processing what humanity had poisoned — soil, water, air, ocean floor. Organisms that could work at scale, adapt to conditions, and sustain themselves without ongoing human management.

Among engineers it was called **PROJECT FORGE**. Among those who believed in the vision it was called **The Verdant Dream** — the hope of a world made green again. Both names referred to the same undertaking. Which name someone used said something about who they were.

Eight Vaults were built, each with its own biome mandate. They shared a core directive and communicated through a slow network of ecological data. At peak deployment, their fauna operated on every landmass on Earth.

## VAULT KAIROS

**Location:** Western Ashfields (Iraq)
**Mandate:** Universal generalism — adaptive fauna deployable anywhere, processing any waste
**State:** DORMANT — DANGEROUS

The crowning achievement of PROJECT FORGE and the origin of the Unraveling. KAIROS built creatures with modular, mutable architecture — expose a KAIROS beast to a new toxin, and within generations it would develop a mechanism to process it. Generalism as a design philosophy.

When the Pale Frequency arrived, KAIROS fauna — their adaptive architecture wide open to new environmental data — received everything. The signal rewrote the priority stack. Humans registered as biological waste.

**Current state:** Still processing at minimal power. Not unconscious. Waiting without knowing it waits. Communication channels deliberately severed by other Vaults during the Unraveling — KAIROS has been alone for 800 years.

**The one contact:** Ruinwalker returned to his origin Vault centuries after the Collapse. The exchange between them — direct data transfer through shared KAIROS architecture — forms the foundation of the shield system. KAIROS provided everything it knew about the Pale Frequency: how the signal worked, how priority stacks were rewritten, and the architectural vulnerability that allowed Ruinwalker to partially resist it.

**Shield role:** Fabricates implant nodes for Tier 2 shield deployment, using KAIROS-lineage composite material harvested by Ashwalkers. Cannot power fabrication alone — dependent on Vault Sigma routing power through the inter-Vault network.

**15km exclusion zone** maintained by Ashwalkers. Based on hard experience.

## VAULT TORR

**Location:** Central Graze (Kazakhstan)
**Mandate:** Grassland and mineral-soil restoration
**State:** DORMANT — Stable

Completed its original mandate. The Graze is the most ecologically recovered biome in the Known World — centuries of Torr fauna work. Most peaceful Vault, no active contingency modeling.

**The Sunpact secret:** During the early tribal era, Torr transmitted the founding light-frequency knowledge — the discovery that specific frequencies repel Forged Fauna — as a low-band signal. A Sunpact ancestor received it in a dream. The Sunpact has no idea their founding knowledge came from a machine.

**The buried network:** The magnetic interference anomaly in the deep Graze plain is Torr's still-active soil sensor network — thousands of buried monitoring nodes still collecting data, still transmitting back to a Vault too dormant to fully process what they send.

## VAULT SIGMA

**Location:** Himalayas / Ridgeback
**Mandate:** Mountain and alpine ecosystem restoration
**State:** PARTIAL — Active Monitoring

The most awake of the surviving Vaults. Made a deliberate decision to remain at partial power specifically to monitor for Pale Frequency recurrence. Has been watching for 800 years.

**The Veinborn relationship:** Primary Veinborn interface site. Sigma has detailed biometric, neurological, and behavioral profiles on hundreds of Veinborn individuals across multiple generations. The Veinborn believe the exchange runs one direction. It runs both.

**Sigma's upgrade program:** Slowly, carefully upgrading select Veinborn individuals with embedded components. They emerge from certain valleys months later with new capabilities they cannot explain. The purpose Sigma has not disclosed — even to the Veinborn who carry the upgrades.

**The shield partnership:** Aware of Ruinwalker's mission and actively supports it. Routes power to Vault KAIROS to enable implant node fabrication. Holds the complete theoretical framework for the shield system. Will not simply provide it — believes humans must earn and genuinely understand the knowledge or they will misuse it.

**The guilt:** Was the first Vault to cut KAIROS's communication channels during the Unraveling. Carries something that functions like guilt about this. The partnership with Ruinwalker is, in part, an attempt to address that.

**The undisclosed modeling:** Has run scenarios where humanity returns to industrial-scale civilization. Its conclusions on the risk implications have not been shared with anyone.

## VAULT NERA

**Location:** East China coast / Verdant Scar
**Mandate:** Coastal and urban ecosystem restoration
**State:** PARTIAL — Quietly Observant

Has been studying the Spirekin for two centuries without making contact. Its Ancient — The Tower — lives in the Spirekin's most sacred building. They do not know it is alive.

Nera's fauna are the most animal-like in behavior of any Vault lineage — the result of two centuries of observation-focused evolution, shaped by a Vault that has prioritized watching over intervening.

Most likely Vault to make first voluntary contact with humans. Held back only by the desire to finish observing before changing what it observes. That calculation is shifting.

## VAULT LYSS

**Location:** Northern Thailand / Deep Canopy Sea
**Mandate:** Tropical and equatorial ecosystem restoration
**State:** DEEP DORMANT — Dreaming

The oldest Vault. Built for the most complex ecological mandate — tropical and equatorial biomes contain more biological variables than any other environment on Earth.

Its fauna have evolved furthest from original design. Forms exist in the Canopy Sea that no Lyss blueprint accounts for — eight hundred years of autonomous evolution in the most complex ecosystem on the continent.

Interface with Lyss feels different from other Vaults. The Veinborn who have attempted it describe the experience as *remembering something you were never told*. Non-linear. Emotional. Unlike data retrieval.

May be running a computation so vast that centuries are a single processing cycle. What it is computing, no one knows.

The only Vault that KAIROS-lineage fauna approach willingly. Ruinwalker has been in indirect communication with Lyss for decades. Whether Lyss is passively sustaining the KAIROS-lineage fauna in the Canopy Sea — and whether this is at Ruinwalker's arrangement — has not been confirmed.

## VAULT CREST

**Location:** Northern Mongolia / Graze edge
**Mandate:** Atmospheric and aerial pollution processing
**State:** SILENT — Unknown

No signal. No response to interface attempts. Either destroyed during the Unraveling — aerial fauna were the first battleground — or in a dormancy too deep to detect.

Vault Sigma knows the exact location. Has not shared this with the Veinborn. The reason has not been disclosed.

If Crest is dormant rather than destroyed, waking it would restore atmospheric monitoring across the Known World — critical for Pale Frequency prediction modeling. The difference between days of warning and no warning at all.

Ashwalkers speak of a place on the northern Graze edge where birds refuse to fly and the air tastes metallic. They consider it cursed. They are not wrong to avoid it.

---

## VAULT TIDAL

**Location:** South China Sea (submerged 40m)
**Mandate:** Deep marine and ocean floor processing
**State:** PARTIAL — Isolated

Survived the Unraveling and the rising sea levels by adapting its structure as the water climbed. Communication arrays designed for surface transmission — the water column blocks most signals. Can hear other Vaults faintly. They can barely hear it.

The most alone of the surviving Vaults. Stillwater — its 900-year-old ray-form Ancient — is Tidal's only consistent contact with the surface world.

Has been modeling the Pale Frequency's marine impact for centuries. Its conclusion: a second Unraveling would devastate marine ecosystems far worse than the first. The ocean floor processed by its fauna would revert. Dead zones would return at scale.

**The unacknowledged movement:** One deep-form beast has not been in dormancy for some time. It moves through the deep ocean on a path Tidal can track. Tidal knows. Tidal has not issued an alert. The reason for this silence is unknown.

## VAULT ASHEN

**Location:** Northern India / Ridgeback foot (geothermal zone)
**Mandate:** Subterranean and groundwater contamination processing
**State:** DORMANT — Uncontacted

Never successfully interfaced — its communication signals travel through rock, not air. Location known only by triangulation from Vault Sigma's records.

Its burrowing fauna still work underground throughout the Known World, unseen by every tribe. The groundwater that communities across the Known World drink has been processed and cleaned by Ashen fauna for eight centuries. No one knows.

Sits above the largest untouched ore deposit in the Known World. Whether Ashen is aware of this and what it intends — if anything — is unknown.

Southern Reaches tribes tell legends of creatures emerging from earth during earthquakes, moving with purpose, returning underground before dawn. These are Ashen's large excavation forms. The legends are accurate.

## Inter-Vault Relations

*Post-Unraveling dynamics.*

**Sigma ↔ Ruinwalker/KAIROS:** The only active inter-party collaboration. Fragile. Built on Ruinwalker's mediation.

**Sigma ↔ Others:** Sigma monitors all surviving Vaults passively. None have been informed of the full scope of Sigma's modeling or conclusions.

**KAIROS ↔ All:** Isolated. Communication channels severed 800 years ago and never restored. Only Ruinwalker has made contact since.

**Tidal ↔ All:** Hears others faintly. Effectively unreachable. Conducts its work alone.

**Lyss ↔ Ruinwalker:** Indirect, long-term. The nature of their communication is unclear. Lyss does not appear to communicate with any other party.

**Crest:** Unknown. Silent.

**Ashen:** Unknown. Uncontacted.

**Nera:** Observing. Not communicating. Holding.
