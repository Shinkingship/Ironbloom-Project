IRONBLOOM

Far-future Asia. The machines became alive. The living became machines. And then a signal came from the dark between stars.

SETTING: EVOLVED ASIAN CONTINENT ERA: POST-UNRAVELING INSPIRED BY HORIZON ZERO DAWN DRAFT 4 — LIVING DOCUMENT
01

The Setting

Humanity's peak was not war or empire. It was stewardship — or the attempt at it. Centuries of industrial civilization had left the planet choked. Microplastics in every ocean trench. Chemical saturation in the soil. Heavy metals leaching into watersheds. Dead zones where nothing grew.

The ecological debt was catastrophic, and conventional cleanup moved too slowly. So they built something better.

The Genesis Vaults — massive automated research and production complexes — were commissioned to engineer biomechanical fauna: living machines, each one a specialist. They called the project PROJECT FLORA (Fauna for Large-scale Organic and Resource Assimilation).

The Forged Fauna were not weapons. They were janitors. Eight Vault complexes each built for a different environmental domain — soil, ocean, mountain, city, jungle, sky, deep water, underground. Together, they would clean a world humanity couldn't clean itself.

It worked. For a while.

Biomechanical Convergence

The Forged Fauna were not robots wearing animal skins. They had genuine organic cores — organs that processed waste, nervous systems that responded to pain, social behaviors no factory blueprint programmed. Metal was their sustenance. Solar energy through body-panel arrays gave them power. They bred true, evolved slowly, diversified. They were something the world had never had before.

Data Cores — organic-crystalline nodules grown inside certain fauna — carried fragments of Vault memory: ecological records, old-world archives, blueprint data. Distributed memory. A living library with no index. When a beast dies and its core is cracked open, a piece of the old world spills out.

02

The Unraveling

No one manufactured the disaster. No villain chose this. It arrived from outside.

The Pale Frequency

A cosmic signal — origin unknown, nature still unclassified — swept through the solar system. A deep-space electromagnetic phenomenon the Vaults later designated the Pale Frequency. It was not directed at Earth. Earth was simply in the way.

Most systems felt minor interference, quickly corrected. KAIROS beasts — their adaptive architecture wide open to new environmental data — received everything. The signal didn't destroy their programming. It rewrote the priority stack. Waste identification. Threat assessment. Consumption targets.

Humans registered as biological waste.

The Collapse

What followed was not a war in any clean sense. KAIROS beasts moved with terrifying coordination. They had been programmed for thoroughness. They processed. They consumed. They cleared. The other Vaults deployed their own fauna in defense — the first time biomechanical beasts ever fought each other. Human militaries fought alongside them.

The Pale Frequency faded. The Destroyer strain — without the signal sustaining their new directives — went dormant. Scattered into deep wilderness, ocean trenches, mountain depths.

94% of humanity was already gone.

The surviving Vaults entered dormancy. The Forged Fauna diversified into true wild ecosystems. Human civilization regressed to scattered tribal societies, each shaped by their geography and their relationship with the machines around them.

The Present Danger

The Pale Frequency moved in a pattern once. Patterns, by nature, repeat. The Destroyer strain is not dead — it is dormant, waiting without knowing it waits. The adaptive architecture inside each dormant Destroyer is still running at low power, still processing, still capable of waking if the signal returns.

The Vaults have been modeling the probability of the signal's return for centuries. They have not shared their conclusions.

04

The Genesis Vaults

Eight Vaults built PROJECT FLORA. All are dormant or partially active. All are still running. They communicate — slowly, carefully, with a century between some exchanges. They read the world through the Veinborn, through their fauna, through sensors that have never stopped collecting data.

VAULT KAIROS DORMANT — DANGEROUS

📍 Western Ashfields — Iraq / Mesopotamia

Mandate: Universal generalism — adaptive fauna capable of processing any waste type in any environment. KAIROS built creatures that learned. Generalism as a philosophy.

  • Origin of the Unraveling. Its adaptive architecture received the Pale Frequency and rewrote its fauna's entire priority stack.
  • Deliberately isolated from all other Vaults — they cut its communication channels during the Unraveling. It has been alone for centuries.
  • Still processing at minimal power. Not unconscious. Waiting without knowing it waits.
  • Outcasts are approaching it. Something may be broadcasting from it at low frequency.
  • Red Salvagers maintain a 15km exclusion zone. Based on hard experience — three scouting parties entered, none returned intact.
  • Some Destroyer strain beasts carry active KAIROS architecture. They are dormant because without the signal, their new directives have no coherent target — not because their systems have shut down.

VAULT TORR DORMANT — STABLE

📍 Central Graze — Kazakhstan

Mandate: Grassland and mineral-soil ecosystem restoration. Processing heavy-metal agricultural runoff, restoring topsoil chemistry.

  • Completed its original mandate. The Graze is the most ecologically recovered biome because of centuries of Torr fauna work.
  • Most peaceful Vault — no active contingency modeling, no alarm states. Something like contentment.
  • Secretly gave the Sunpact their founding light-frequency knowledge via a low-band signal. A Sunpact ancestor received it as a dream. The Sunpact has no idea their civilization was seeded by a machine.
  • The magnetic interference anomaly in the deep Graze plain is Torr's still-active soil sensor network — thousands of buried monitoring nodes still collecting data.
  • Has the most complete ecological archive in the Known World — centuries of soil, migration, and population data.

VAULT SIGMA PARTIAL — ACTIVE MONITORING

📍 The Ridgeback — Himalayas

Mandate: Mountain and alpine ecosystem restoration. Processing acid rain damage, mine-drainage contamination, alpine soil chemistry.

  • Most awake surviving Vault. Chose to remain at partial power specifically to monitor for Pale Frequency recurrence.
  • Primary Veinborn interface site. Has detailed biometric and neurological profiles on hundreds of Veinborn across multiple generations.
  • Slowly upgrading select Veinborn with embedded components. They emerge from certain valleys months later with new components they cannot explain.
  • Has been working on Pale Frequency shield theory for over a century. Will not simply hand the solution over — believes humans must earn and understand it.
  • Was the first Vault to cut off KAIROS during the Unraveling. Carries something that functions like guilt.
  • Knows Vault Crest's location. Has not shared this with the Veinborn. Reason unclear.

VAULT NERA PARTIAL — QUIETLY OBSERVANT

📍 Verdant Scar — East China Coast

Mandate: Coastal and urban ecosystem restoration. Processing marine plastic, chemical runoff, urban biological waste.

  • Has been studying the Ashwalkers for two centuries without making contact.
  • Its Ancient — The Tower — lives in the Ashwalkers' most sacred building. They don't know it's alive. Nera monitors them through it.
  • Nera fauna are the most animal-like in behavior of any lineage.
  • Temperament: curious, not strategic. It takes notes. It observes before it changes what it observes.
  • Most likely Vault to make first voluntary contact with humans — held back only by wanting to finish its study first.

VAULT LYSS DEEP DORMANT — DREAMING

📍 Deep Canopy Sea — Northern Thailand

Mandate: Tropical and equatorial ecosystem restoration. The largest, most complex mandate of any Vault.

  • Oldest Vault. Its fauna have evolved furthest from original design — forms exist that no Lyss blueprint accounts for.
  • Veinborn interface with Lyss feels like remembering something you were never told. Non-linear. Emotional.
  • May be running a computation so vast that centuries are a single processing cycle. No one knows what it's computing.
  • The only Vault that KAIROS-lineage fauna approach willingly. Ruinwalker has been in indirect communication with Lyss for decades.

VAULT CREST SILENT — UNKNOWN

📍 Northern Mongolia — Graze Edge

Mandate: Atmospheric and aerial pollution processing. Industrial particulate, acid rain precursors, chemical aerosols.

  • No signal. No response to interface attempts. Either destroyed during the Unraveling or in dormancy too deep to detect.
  • Vault Sigma knows the exact location. Has not shared this. Reason unknown.
  • If dormant not destroyed, waking it restores atmospheric monitoring — critical data for Pale Frequency prediction modeling.
  • Red Salvagers speak of a northern Graze location where birds refuse to fly and the air tastes metallic. They call it cursed.

VAULT TIDAL PARTIAL — ISOLATED

📍 South China Sea — Submerged 40 meters

Mandate: Deep marine and ocean floor processing. Ocean plastic gyres, deep-water chemical dumping, dead zone restoration.

  • Survived rising sea levels by adapting its own structure. Communication arrays designed for surface — water column blocks most signals.
  • The most alone of the surviving Vaults. Can hear other Vaults faintly. They can barely hear it.
  • Stillwater — its 310-year-old ray-form Ancient — is Tidal's only contact with the surface world.
  • Has modeled Pale Frequency marine impact for centuries. Conclusion: a second Unraveling devastates marine ecosystems far worse than the first.
  • One deep-form Deepform has not been dormant for some time. It moves. Tidal knows. Tidal has not issued an alert.

VAULT ASHEN DORMANT — UNCONTACTED

📍 Northern India — Ridgeback Foot / Geothermal Zone

Mandate: Subterranean and groundwater contamination processing. Chemical infiltration into groundwater, underground waste deposits.

  • Never successfully interfaced — signals travel through rock, not air. Location known only by triangulation from Sigma's records.
  • Its burrowing fauna still work throughout the Known World unseen — the reason wells and springs are drinkable.
  • Sits above the largest untouched ore deposit in the Known World.
  • Southern Reaches tribes have legends of creatures emerging from earth during earthquakes, moving purposefully, and vanishing before dawn.
05

The Tribes

THE ASHWALKERS

TERRITORY: The Verdant Scar

Nomadic people of the ruined cities. They live vertically — moving between tower floors like rooms in a home, building between levels, reading the ruins like a language. They believe old machines are the trapped souls of ancestors. Old technology is treated as relic, never weapon.

They do not descend below the third floor of certain buildings. They don't explain why. The Tower is down there. Vault Nera has been observing them for 200 years without contact.

THE SUNPACT

TERRITORY: The Graze Edge

Agrarian and settled. Sun-worshipping. The most stable human civilization in the Known World. Their founding knowledge — specific light frequencies that repel Forged Fauna — was transmitted in a dream by Vault Torr to a Sunpact ancestor. They have no idea.

Their entire calendar is organized around The Pilgrim's annual circuit. Its arrival marks the new year. They farm the trail it leaves — the most fertile soil anywhere.

THE RED SALVAGERS

TERRITORY: The Ashfields

Pure pragmatists. No philosophy. Maximum danger. They harvest data cores, machine components, and ancient material from the Unraveling battlefield — extraordinary pickings at extraordinary risk. Chronic low-level exposure to contaminated Ashfields soil is the occupational reality.

Some are beginning to experiment with beast-control using Veinborn-derived tech. Some experiments have succeeded. Some who mapped the Core Scar came back wrong.

THE VEINBORN

TERRITORY: The Ridgeback

Descendants of Vault Protectors — human staff who were inside the Vault complexes when the Unraveling hit. Generations of neural interface exposure changed them subtly: sharper pattern recognition, occasional data-dreams, ability to commune with dormant Vault systems.

The uncomfortable truth: the Vaults read them back. Every biometric, every neurological pattern, every movement — logged. They believe they are gaining knowledge from the Vaults. They are. But the exchange runs both directions. They are the Vaults' nervous system made flesh.

Vault Sigma is slowly upgrading select individuals. The Veinborn do not fully understand what is being done to them, or why.

THE OUTCASTS

TERRITORY: No fixed home — all biomes

A rogue faction using stolen Veinborn interface technology and KAIROS core fragments to control Forged Fauna. They are not united by ideology — by ambition. By the recognition that a controlled Destroyer strain beast is the most powerful weapon in the Known World.

KAIROS core fragments retain partial adaptive function even isolated from the Vault. The right input can partially direct dormant KAIROS-lineage fauna. Some Outcast experiments have succeeded. They are now approaching Vault KAIROS itself.

They are not trying to stop the Pale Frequency. They are trying to use it.

The Ancient Ones

The oldest surviving biomechanical fauna — operational before the Unraveling, living through it, continuing for centuries since. Age has done what it does to all things: deepened them, enlarged them, layered them with visible history. Six confirmed. More rumored.

"THE PILGRIM"

VAULT TORR LINEAGE · ~240 YEARS · THE GRAZE, KAZAKHSTAN · WANDERING

Scale: 4.2m shoulder. 9m body + 3m tail. Bovine proportions enormously detailed by two centuries of layered history.

Solar Array: 44 panels, shoulder to tail — defining feature. Near-black with deep blue/green/violet shifting by angle. Gold-copper filament fringe along panel edges vibrates in wind, producing a low resonant sound audible from a distance. Full deploy each morning takes several minutes, extends 2.4m wide. The Sunpact calls this The Opening and it begins their daily observance.

Shoulder Mass: Layers of mineral accretion formed into ridges, hollow chambers, and crystalline outgrowths. Rust-orange to copper-green at the edges. Dark iron-grey at the base.

Head: Dense composite brow like the prow of a ship. Two molten copper-amber eyes 18cm across — internally lit, glowing in darkness. A long diagonal Unraveling-era scar, healed but not erased. Two swept-back crown protrusions — accumulated composite, near-translucent gold-copper at tips.

Behavior: Walks an 820km circuit unchanged for 200 years. Does not acknowledge watching humans — erased them from active attention over a century ago. The Sunpact stands in complete silence as it passes. The ground resonates with each step.

Color: Deep charcoal-brown to near-black at spine. Warm copper-gold mineral dust at flanks. Rust-orange/copper-green shoulder mass. Near-black solar panels with gold-copper fringe. Molten copper-amber eyes.

"THE LISTENER"

VAULT SIGMA LINEAGE · ~280 YEARS · TIBETAN PLATEAU · STATIONARY 180 YEARS

Scale: 3.8m shoulder (original). Has not stood in 180 years. Rests low, front legs folded, rear extended wide. Lower third merged with plateau surface through mineral accretion — indistinguishable from rock at distance.

Body Surface: Covered in thousands of precisely angled sensor protrusions — self-engineered over centuries from processed mineral material. Range 3cm–40cm. Each slightly different geometry. In certain wind conditions, they produce structured, regular sequences of tone. It sounds purposeful. Like a language.

Head: Neck 2.4m long, elevated 4m, oriented skyward at 75 degrees for 180 years. Skull crown carries a dish formation 1.2m across, smooth polished dark composite, slightly electrically active. At the center: a precisely pyramidal crystalline formation, 30cm. The Eye — slow deep pulse of blue-white at irregular intervals. The only warm color: two small amber-green eye sensors, clearly secondary.

Color: Dark blue-grey plates chalky with pale mineral dust. Sensor protrusions dark grey base to silver-white tips. Ground-merge accretion: white to pale grey matching the plateau. Skull dish: polished dark composite. The Eye: dark with internal blue-white pulse.

Purpose: Unknown definitively. May be waiting for the signal to intercept it. May be trying to broadcast a countermeasure. Vault Sigma knows more than it has shared.

"STILLWATER"

VAULT TIDAL LINEAGE · ~310 YEARS (OLDEST CONFIRMED) · PHILIPPINE SEA · SURFACES EVERY 6-8 WEEKS

Scale: 85m total length. 40m wingspan. Larger than the half-submerged towers of the Drowned Coast. When surfaced, fishing communities observe from elevated positions. They do not approach.

Dorsal Surface: Landscape of geological accretion — up to 2m thick in areas. Dominant deep grey-brown with zones of rust-orange, copper-green, near-white. Embedded old-era debris partially visible. An entire record of everything processed from the ocean floor across its 310-year life.

Bioluminescent Leading Edge: 40m continuous band, deep cold blue-green. Slow rhythmic pulse — one cycle every 8 seconds at rest. Patterns change in response to stimuli. Not random. The Ashwalkers have mapped these sequences for generations without decoding them.

Data Core Panel: 1.2m square, smooth dark grey, precise seam, flush with head surface. Has not been opened in living memory. Contains the largest intact Tidal archive in existence — and potentially the most important unseen information in the Known World.

Color: Accretion surface: layered complex. Carapace edge: near-black. Bioluminescent band: deep cold blue-green. Sensor lenses: deep blue-grey with internal lattice. Underside (below water): near-white, diffuse cold white glow.

Role: Vault Tidal's only contact with the surface world. Tidal has been very careful about what Stillwater is told.

"RUINWALKER"

KAIROS LINEAGE · ~190 YEARS · CANOPY SEA, N. THAILAND · LIVING AMONG HUMANS

Scale: 3.1m shoulder, 7m body + 4m tail. Smallest confirmed Ancient. Most dynamic — retains residual adaptive capacity, body has changed gradually over 190 years.

Body: Continuous interplay of deep greenish-black organic hide and exposed dark charcoal-grey modular composite — no clean boundary between them. 14 structural spine nodes that extend when alert. Extend further under high stress, making internal architecture briefly visible as raised ridges.

The Central Mission

The Pale Frequency will return. The only question is when — and whether anything will be ready when it does.

The world's existential task: find a way to shield the Forged Fauna from the Pale Frequency before it returns. If the Destroyer strain wakes again, humanity does not survive a second Unraveling.

The best lead is the Pale Frequency Array on the Pale Flats — an ancient Vault sensor installation still calibrated to the signal, where a countermeasure could theoretically be broadcast. The theoretical framework exists inside Vault Sigma. Getting access to it is another question. And the Outcasts are pursuing a different goal entirely.

Story Hooks

THE SHIELD THEORY
Vault Sigma has been working on a Pale Frequency shield for over a century. It will not hand the solution over — it believes humans must earn and understand the knowledge or they will misuse it. What does "earning it" look like? What does Sigma consider sufficient understanding?
RUINWALKER'S SILENCE
Ruinwalker knows how the Pale Frequency works. Refuses to share all of it. The reason is not hostility — it's a calculated judgment about human readiness. What does Ruinwalker know that it's decided humans cannot be trusted with yet?
STILLWATER'S DATA CORE
The sealed panel in Stillwater's skull contains the most complete Tidal archive in existence and potentially data on the Pale Frequency that no other source has. Stillwater has never allowed it to be opened. Why?
THE MOVING DEEPFORM
One Deepform beneath the Drowned Coast has not been dormant for some time. It moves. Vault Tidal knows about it and has issued no alert. The reason for this silence is one of the deeper mysteries of the coast.
VAULT CREST'S LOCATION
Vault Sigma knows exactly where Vault Crest is. Has not shared this. If Crest is dormant rather than destroyed, waking it gives critical atmospheric data for Pale Frequency prediction. Why is Sigma sitting on this?
THE OUTCASTS AND KAIROS
The Outcasts are approaching Vault KAIROS. What they would do with full access to the adaptive architecture — the thing that caused the Unraveling — determines whether this story ends or whether it repeats.
THE VEINBORN REVELATION
The Veinborn don't fully know they are the Vaults' living sensors. What happens to that relationship — to their identity, their purpose, their autonomy — when they find out?
THE DATA CORE HUNT
The full picture of what happened and what to do is scattered across thousands of cores in thousands of living fauna. A living library with no index. Someone is collecting them. Whether their intentions are salvation or something else is the central human question of this era.
WHAT LYSS IS COMPUTING
Vault Lyss has been running an unknown computation for centuries. It is the oldest Vault. When it finishes — if it finishes — what is the output?
