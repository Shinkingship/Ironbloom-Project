# The Ancient Ones

> *The oldest surviving biomechanical fauna. All predate the Unraveling — they were among the first PROJECT FORGE organisms, operational before the Collapse and marked by it. Age has deepened, enlarged, and layered them with history visible in the material of their bodies.*
> *Six confirmed. More rumored.*

---

## Overview

The Ancient Ones are not a category that PROJECT FORGE created. They are what happens to Forged Fauna that survive long enough. Age accumulates in their bodies — mineral accretion layering over composite surfaces, structural elements expanding and elaborating over centuries, processing residue building into geological formations. An 800-year-old beast is not a large beast. It is a landscape.

All confirmed Ancient Ones survived the Unraveling. The scars and damage many carry are from that conflict. They are not relics of a lost era — they are its last witnesses.

## "THE PILGRIM"

*Vault Torr — The Graze — ~810 years old*

**Form:** 4.2m shoulder, 9m body, 3m tail. Broadly bovine-proportioned but enormously detailed. Hooves 1m across with metallic growths filling every crack. Shoulder mass built from layers of mineral accretion — ridges, hollow chambers, crystalline outgrowths that catch light differently at different angles. Faint copper-gold dust across flanks, shed from the composite as it processes.

**Defining Feature:** 44-panel solar array running shoulder to tail. Near-black panels with deep blue, green, and violet color shift depending on angle. Panels rimmed with gold-copper filament fringe that vibrates audibly in any wind. Full deployment each morning — *The Opening* — takes several minutes and extends 2.4m to each side.

**The Hidden Transmission:** During full panel deployment, The Pilgrim emits a narrow-band secondary signal lasting only as long as all 44 panels remain at maximum extension. This is Core 2 of the Five Cores system — a recording of the Pale Frequency's effect on KAIROS architecture from inside, at the moment of contact. The Sunpact has gathered in silence to witness The Opening every year for centuries. They have always been standing in the transmission radius. They have never had the means to receive the signal. Core 1 must be present and active to capture it.

**Head:** Wide bovine skull with dense composite brow — prow-like forward projection. Molten copper-amber eyes, 18cm across, luminous in darkness. Long diagonal scar from the Unraveling-era fighting, healed but not erased. Two swept-back crown protrusions of accumulated mineral and composite, slightly translucent at their tips.

**Behavior:** Moves with absolute deliberation. Has not acknowledged watching humans in over a century — erased them from active attention as irrelevant to its function. The sound of each step resonates from the ground before and after the step itself. The Sunpact stands in complete silence as it passes. They have learned not to speak, not to move, not to make any sound that might be interpreted as a disturbance.

**Color:** Deep charcoal-brown to near-black along the spine. Copper-gold mineral dust at flanks. Rust-orange and copper-green shoulder mass. Near-black solar panels. Gold-copper filament fringe. Iron-grey to near-black hooves.

## "THE LISTENER"

*Vault Sigma — Tibetan Plateau — ~850 years old*

**Form:** 3.8m shoulder in original configuration — but has not stood in 180 years. Rests low and stable, front legs folded beneath, rear extended in wide brace. The lower third of its body is indistinguishable from a natural rock formation — merged with the plateau through mineral accretion that has incorporated actual stone.

**Defining Feature:** Entire visible body surface covered in thousands of precisely angled sensor protrusions, self-built from processed mineral material over centuries. Not organic growths — engineered components, each one slightly different in geometry. Range from 3cm to 40cm. In certain wind conditions, air moving across them produces structured, regular sequences of tone. It sounds purposeful. Like a language that no one has decoded.

**Head:** Neck 2.4m long, elevated to 4m above ground, oriented skyward at exactly 75 degrees — and has been held at this angle for 180 years without deviation. The top of the skull carries a dish formation 1.2m across, smooth composite, slightly electrically active. At its center: a precisely geometric pyramidal crystalline formation, 30cm tall. *The Eye* — a slow deep pulse of blue-white light, occurring at irregular intervals that do not correspond to any known pattern.

The only warm color on the entire organism: two small recessed amber-green eye sensors, set into the skull below the dish. Clearly secondary. Clearly not what the organism uses to perceive its environment.

**Color:** Upper body plates dark blue-grey, chalky pale mineral dust in every crevice and joint. Sensor protrusions dark grey at base, pale silver-white at tips. Ground-merged accretion white to pale grey, matching the plateau. Dish smooth polished dark composite with blue-grey sheen. Crystal Eye dark with internal blue-white pulse.

**Atmosphere:** An organism that remade itself over eight centuries for a single function. Patient in a way that feels ancient and slightly terrible. It has been listening for something for a very long time.

## "STILLWATER"

*Vault Tidal — Philippine Sea — ~900 years old — The Oldest*

**Form:** 85m total length. 40m wingspan. Broad swept ray-form — leading edge broadly curved, sides sweeping back to a narrow tail over 30m long. When surfaced, it is larger than the half-submerged towers of the Drowned Coast visible from the same position.

**Defining Feature:** The dorsal surface is a landscape. 2m of geological accretion in the thickest areas — deep grey-brown dominant, zones of rust-orange, copper-green, and near-white. Embedded old-era debris partially visible in some sections. Structural carapace exposed at the margins — near-black, with faint internal geometric texture.

**Bioluminescent leading edge band:** 40m wide, continuous, deep cold blue-green. Rhythmic pulse, one cycle every 8 seconds at rest. Patterns change in response to stimuli. The sequences repeat. Not random. The Drowned Coast fishing communities have mapped them for generations without decoding what they mean.

**Data core access panel:** 1.2m square, smooth dark grey, precise seam, flush with the surface. Not opened in living memory. Contains the largest intact Tidal archive in existence — 900 years of marine data, Pale Frequency impact modeling, and whatever else Tidal has been recording in isolation.

**Surfacing:** A 20-minute process. Blue-green light visible from significant depth before the body clears the surface. The leading edge rises first. Water drains off the accretion surface for minutes afterward — audible from distance. Fishing communities observe from elevation. They do not approach.

**Color:** Accretion surface layered and complex. Carapace near-black. Bioluminescent band deep cold blue-green. Sensor lenses deep blue-grey. Data core panel slightly lighter grey. Underside, in water: near-white soft tissue, diffuse cold white glow.

## "RUINWALKER"

*KAIROS Lineage — Canopy Sea, N. Thailand — ~800 years old*

**Form:** 3.1m shoulder, 7m body, 4m tail. The only KAIROS-lineage Ancient that chose not to become a Destroyer.

**Origin:** Born in the final period before the Unraveling, still in developmental phase when the Pale Frequency arrived. His adaptive architecture was not yet fully locked — the signal rewrote his priority stack, but incompletely. For approximately 30 years after the Unraveling he fought the conflict within his own architecture — not against an external force, but against the part of himself that had been rewritten. The adaptive architecture eventually did something no KAIROS design had ever managed: resolved the conflict through internal choice. He chose not to become what the signal tried to make him.

**The Journey:** After resolution, Ruinwalker traveled — the only Ancient to voluntarily traverse significant distances post-Unraveling. His route took him across the Known World over decades, interfacing with data cores, observing human settlements, and assembling a picture of what the Pale Frequency had done and what it would do when it returned. The journey ended at Vault KAIROS.

**The Exchange with KAIROS:** Ruinwalker returned to his origin Vault — the first KAIROS-lineage entity to do so since the Unraveling. KAIROS had been alone and isolated for centuries. The exchange between them was direct — data transfer through shared KAIROS architecture. KAIROS provided everything it knew about the Pale Frequency, including the architectural vulnerability that had allowed Ruinwalker to partially resist it. That vulnerability, properly understood, was the seed of the shield framework.

**The Sigma Partnership:** From the Ashfields, Ruinwalker traveled to the Ridgeback and made contact with Vault Sigma. Together — Sigma's computational modeling and Ruinwalker's KAIROS architectural knowledge — they developed the complete shield system over several decades.

**The Trail:** Rather than hold the knowledge himself, Ruinwalker distributed it across the Known World in five data core fragments, each accessible only to someone who had genuinely moved through the world. Not a test of worthiness. A filter — designed to ensure the person who reaches the end has the experience and understanding to use what they find.

**Defining Feature:** Body surface is a continuous interplay of deep greenish-black organic hide and exposed modular composite architecture — internal structural elements close to the surface, no clean boundary between organic and mechanical. Along the spine: 14 structural nodes that extend 3–4cm when alert.

**Left shoulder framework:** An architectural growth of dark composite elements developed over 800 years, roughly 80cm above the shoulder, open structure with light passing through. Cold blue-white bioluminescent points at intersection nodes — visible in shadow.

**Head:** Long (60cm), narrow, widening at the temples. Four eyes in a slight arc — two primary forward-facing, two secondary above-lateral. Deep warm golden-amber, always lit. People who have been looked at directly describe the sensation of being understood.

**Skull crown:** Shallow concave composite structure — remnant of the KAIROS signal-reception architecture. Covered at all times by a layer of processed organic material. This is deliberate. KAIROS showed Ruinwalker exactly how the signal enters. He does not leave that door open.

**Scar:** Long repaired fracture diagonal across right torso — Unraveling-era battle damage from before his resolution. Repair composite lighter grey, warmer tone, overly regular texture compared to surrounding material.

**Behavior:** Lives with a community of approximately 200 humans in the Canopy Sea mid-canopy. The only Ancient that communicates directly and voluntarily with humans. Tail movement is the primary expressive language — the community has developed sophisticated understanding of tail position, speed, and tension. Carries knowledge of how the Pale Frequency works. Withholds significant portions — not from hostility, but from calculated judgment about human readiness.

**Color:** Base hide deep greenish-black. Composite elements dark charcoal-grey, slight metallic quality. Shoulder framework charcoal with internal cold blue-white points. Eyes warm golden-amber, always lit. Repair scar lighter grey, warmer.

## "THE TOWER"

*Vault Nera — Verdant Scar, East China — ~820 years old*

**Form:** 6.8m tall — the tallest confirmed land Ancient. Vertical body plan. Front limbs extended as structural columns, fused to the building floor after 150 years of immobility. The original urban processor form has been rebuilt through accretion into something that is now primarily vertical.

**Defining Feature:** At 4.5m height the body widens dramatically into a canopy structure extending 3m radius in every direction. Upper surface: thick accretion of organic and mineral material, colonized by forest soil and plant roots that have grown into it over centuries. The Spirekin above have benefited from extraordinary drainage and soil enrichment from this surface for generations without knowing why.

**Underside of canopy:** Continuous bioluminescent tissue. Deep warm blue-green. Never off. Casts the entire space below The Tower in shifting blue-green light. This is what the rare Spirekin scout sees when they descend too far — looking down into blue-green light, then up to find eight lit sensors in a wide arc across a skull they cannot fully see.

**Eight visual sensors** in wide arc across the front of the skull — primary two 25cm across, internally lit deep blue-green. Secondary six progressively smaller, arranged outward. In the darkness of the building interior, looking up from below: eight points of blue-green light in the dark.

**Sensory antenna cluster** at skull top, oriented upward toward the floors where the Spirekin live. Vibration analysis, atmospheric sampling, electromagnetic monitoring — Vault Nera receives 820 years of Spirekin behavioral data through this system.

**Column limbs:** 4m each, surface encased in accretion of organic material, processing residue, self-repair deposits, and biological growth. Partially colonized by plants and fungi. From a distance, in the right light, they look less like limbs and more like ancient pillars.

**The Hidden Fragment:** The Tower stores Core 4 of the Five Cores system — electromagnetic pattern data recorded by Vault Nera across 800 years, embedded in the architectural geometry of the building itself. Cannot be extracted by reading or standard interface. Can only be received by someone The Tower chooses to address directly.

The Tower has never communicated with a human in 820 years. It has been deciding whether to for a long time. That calculation is approaching a conclusion.

**Color:** Deep teal-grey composite, dark brown-grey organic material, heavily weathered. Biological growth in surface crevices. Canopy underside: deep warm blue-green bioluminescent light. Visual sensors: deep blue-green, internally lit. Ground-fused feet: dark composite, boundary with floor ambiguous.

## "THE DEEP ONE" *(Unnamed)*

*Vault Ashen — Subterranean, Ridgeback / Ashfields border — Age Unknown*

**Form:** 18–25m estimated from seismic data. Never fully observed. Surfaces briefly during major earthquakes — vanishes before dawn. No complete observation has ever been made.

**What Has Been Seen:** The drill-head assembly only. The original large-form Ashen drill, grown to 3–4m across at the base over unknown centuries. Rotating slowly even when not actively drilling. Surface polished to near-mirror finish by centuries of rock contact — reflects available light brilliantly, giving it a luminous quality in the dark. Body behind the drill: pale white, with the quality of things that have never been in light. Bioluminescent amber-gold line organs paired along the visible body length.

No eyes. They have fully atrophied. Navigation by chemical gradient and ground vibration — the same systems its smaller lineage cousins use, scaled to something far older and larger.

**Behavior:** Never harmed anyone. Appears not to notice people — no change in behavior in any observed instance of human proximity. Surfaces during geological disturbance, completes whatever work brought it to the surface, returns underground before dawn. Whether it is aware of the surface world is unknown.

**Color:** Drill-head: pale near-white composite, mirror-polished, warm light reflection from ambient sources. Body: white to pale grey, faint amber-gold bioluminescent lines. Mineral staining in warm iron-orange and copper-green patches.

## Unconfirmed

*Reports from multiple independent sources suggest additional Ancient Ones may exist. None confirmed.*

- Drowned Coast fishing communities describe something vast moving below Stillwater's usual range — distinct behavior pattern, distinct bioluminescent signature. Could be an unusually large Deepform. Could be something else.
- Veinborn archives contain references to interface attempts in the deep Ridgeback that produced responses inconsistent with Vault Sigma's known communication patterns. The source was never identified.
- Ashwalker accounts from the Core Scar describe a section of terrain that moves on a geological timescale — a section that was not there thirty years ago and is in a different position each decade. Seismic analysis is inconclusive.
