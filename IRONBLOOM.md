## World Summary

**Setting:** Far-future Asia. Approximately 800 years after the Unraveling.

**The Age That Was:** Industrial civilization choked the planet. PROJECT FORGE — known to believers as *The Verdant Dream* — commissioned the Genesis Vaults to engineer biomechanical organisms capable of consuming what humanity could not clean. Eight Vaults. Eight mandates. One shared directive.

**The Unraveling:** A cosmic signal of unknown origin swept through the solar system. KAIROS-lineage fauna — their adaptive architecture wide open to new data — received everything. The signal rewrote their priority stack. Humans registered as biological waste. 94% of humanity was gone before the signal faded and the Destroyer strain went dormant.

**The World Now:** Scattered tribal societies. Forged Fauna evolved into true ecosystems. The Vaults entered dormancy. Human civilization never recovered beyond small, mobile communities — held at that scale by an ecological dynamic they do not understand and may not have chosen.

**The Threat:** The Pale Frequency moved in a pattern once. Patterns repeat. Almost no one knows. The knowledge exists in fragments, distributed deliberately across the Known World, waiting for someone to follow the trail.

## The Central Tension

The shield exists as theory. The knowledge to build it is distributed and waiting. Almost no one knows the threat is real. The people most capable of stopping it do not trust each other. The people trying to exploit it do not understand what they are playing with.

The Pale Frequency will return. The only question is whether anything is ready.
